## Description (4000 chars max)

BenchGoblins is a fantasy sports decision engine that answers your toughest lineup questions. Ask who to start, who to sit, whether to accept a trade, or who to grab off waivers — and get a clear, reasoned answer in seconds.

Unlike prediction tools that promise point totals, BenchGoblins treats fantasy as what it actually is: a decision problem under uncertainty. Every recommendation explains the reasoning behind it so you can make smarter choices, not just follow rankings blindly.

HOW IT WORKS

Type your question in plain English:
- "Should I start Jokic or Embiid tonight?"
- "Drop Conner for Pacheco?"
- "Trade Mahomes for Jefferson straight up?"

BenchGoblins evaluates your options using five factors: space creation, role stability, defensive attention, opportunity trends, and matchup fit. You get a clear recommendation with the reasoning laid out — not a number pulled from thin air.

CHOOSE YOUR RISK MODE

Every decision depends on your situation. Select the mode that fits your week:
- FLOOR — Minimize disaster. Prioritize safe, stable options.
- MEDIAN — Maximize expected value. The balanced default.
- CEILING — Swing for upside. Accept volatility for spike weeks.

FIVE SPORTS, ONE ENGINE

- NBA (primary) — Full coverage with deep matchup analysis
- NFL — Start/sit, trades, and waiver wire decisions
- MLB (beta) — Batting order context and pitching matchups
- NHL (beta) — Line combinations and special teams impact
- Soccer (beta) — FPL, La Liga, UCL, and MLS with fixture difficulty analysis

WHAT YOU GET FREE

- 5 decisions per day
- All 5 sports
- All three risk modes

BENCHGOBLINS PRO

- Unlimited decisions
- All five sports (NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, Soccer)
- Priority response times

## App Review Notes (for Apple reviewers)

BenchGoblins is a fantasy sports decision tool. Users type questions about their fantasy sports lineups ("Should I start Player A or Player B?") and receive analysis-backed recommendations.

Free tier: 5 decisions/day, all 5 sports (NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, Soccer).
Pro tier: Unlimited decisions, advanced AI analysis, all sports.

Demo credentials are not required — the app works without login for free-tier usage.

The app does NOT provide gambling, betting, or odds information. It is a decision-support tool for fantasy sports league managers.
